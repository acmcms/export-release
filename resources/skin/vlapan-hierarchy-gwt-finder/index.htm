About

Hello, I'm com.vlapan.gwt.Finder widget. You can use me in forms or in your favorite rich editor. I help you choose any object in deepest hierarchy. I perfect link chooser.

Look at me, I am nice, isn't it? Please play with me!

I can get data from HTML page where I have placed, and by remote request.

So if you have a little amount of data you can put it in page, I will show it as fast as I can.

But also you can feed me a first part of data in page, and then if I need more I request it.

You can feed me like that
